After Round One Classic
RULES
    Welcome to After Round One. This is a two player game: You and I. To play the game,
  1. Choose a number from 1 to 10 other than the number chosen by me. We will call this your "goal"
  2. When the game starts, you are to play numbers from 0 to 5. I will do the same
  3. Your chosen number is added to the number I play
  4. If the sum is equal to your goal, then you win; if its equal to my goal,I win.
  5. The game continues if no ones goal is reached
